Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. At AnonWipe, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. No File Storage

We do not store your files. All files you upload are processed in-memory or in a temporary directory and are deleted immediately after processing. No copies are kept on our servers.

2. No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party tracking services. Your activity on AnonWipe is not tracked or monitored.

3. No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your files or any personal information to any third parties.

4. Security

We use secure protocols to ensure your files are transmitted safely. All processing is done on our secure servers and files are deleted after processing.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at test@anonwipe.com.
